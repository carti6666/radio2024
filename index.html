SCA1 (Side A) SCA(SoundSupply_Service Compilation Album) Project is our compilation album project.

We aim to bring various talented artists in various genres together to present their music via planning, producing, and releasing compilation albums with those artists. ‘SCA1(Side A)’ is our first release of the SCA Project. ‘SCA1(Side A)’ consists of 8 tracks made by 8 various producers/teams. All featuring artists are based in Seoul. We brought them together to introduce their music. SCA Project albums are released in the 2nd and 4th quarters of each year.
